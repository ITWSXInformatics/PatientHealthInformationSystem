ABOUT

RPI Xinformatics Health Information Portal is developed by:

  • Bowen Gong, Harsh Sugandh, Priyanshu Tripathi, Siwen Zhang, Vrishti Jain
  • Thilanka Munasinghe

The users can use this platform to create or record their heart rate data, and share data to United HealthCare.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF HEALTH INFORMATION PORTAL?


Patients & Caregives

Empowers patients to better manage their health and actively participate in their health care.

Clinicians

Provides a holistic view of patients' health over time and enables shared decidsion-making about care plans.

Researchers

Provides access to more expansive and diverse datasets to aid in clinical research.
